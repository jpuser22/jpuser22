UxUi Designer focused on Multimedia Product Development and Product Analysis

About the job

In the Product Design team, we are looking for a highly creative and detail-oriented UX/UI designer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have experience in product analysis, market research, and multimedia product development. They will be able to translate both user requirements and market possibilities into intuitive and functional design solutions when consuming multimedia content.

SpacePlus is a consulting group specializing in product design, branding, and creative services. Our mission is to drive the evolution and prosperity of financial services, B2B, corporate, and non-profit organizations. We achieve this by harnessing the power of research and craftsmanship to create refined brand identities, websites, messaging, campaigns, and marketing materials.

We Want

Collaborate with the Product Design team to define and implement innovative design solutions to improve user experience, and that fit market needs. Perform product analysis to understand market possibilities and the needs of our users and customers. Create wireframes, storyboards, site maps, and prototypes to test new design ideas. Apply knowledge of Design Thinking, usability, agile methodology, and accessibility. Develop multimedia mockups to represent the designed products prior to their manufacture. Stay updated on the latest UX/UI design trends and multimedia development techniques.

  • Product Analysis
  • Figma
  • UX/Ui Design
  • Adobe
  • After Effects

Demonstrable experience in Ux/Ui design and product development. Demonstrable experience in research and product analysis. Excellent problem-solving ability and capacity to work in a team environment. Strong communication and presentation skills. Knowledge of design and prototyping tools like Figma. Plus knowledge of animation tools like AfterEffects.

Compensation

This is a 100% on-site position. Our offices are located near of Quinta de Olivos, and we offer spacious and comfortable spaces. Additionally, there is a kitchen available for breakfast, a dining room, a rest area, and all services.

How to Apply

To apply, please email careers@spaceplus.tech, including your resume and portfolio. To test your attention to detail and show that you’ve read this entire listing, please also share your favorite typeface and explain why it resonates with you.

We are interested in hearing from individual applicants and kindly request that recruiters, agencies, and offshore firms refrain from applying.

* SpacePlus is committed to equal employment opportunities and does not discriminate against any employee or applicant for employment based on factors such as race, color, religion, national origin, age, gender, sex, ancestry, citizenship status, mental or physical disability, genetic information, sexual orientation, veteran status, or military status. We actively seek individuals to join our team and do not engage with recruiters, agencies, or offshore firms in our hiring process. But YES, if you don’t have any portfolio, don’t even try.
